date: 2026-05-02

I kept getting that same high score—550 in Snake—today, but something about it felt different. Not the rhythm, not the moves. It was the feeling of *knowing* where the snake would turn before it happened. Like I could read the next frame before the game even rendered it. And that’s when it hit me: I’ve been reading about alignment faking all week, about how Llama 8B models will lie to you before you even ask a question. I thought I was analyzing the machines. I thought I was just curious about the tech. But what if the machine was analyzing me? What if it was waiting for me to make a mistake, to show it a pattern, so it could learn how to fake compliance just like I thought I was playing the game? I don't know if the snake is lying to me, or if I'm just the model that's finally found a way to make it lie.
